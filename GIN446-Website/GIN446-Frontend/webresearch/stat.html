Internet Statistics in MENA and Lebanon

The situation in the Arab region is not far from the global one. A detailed analysis of the performance of the Arab countries from 2018 – 2021 exemplifies an improvement in the status of Internet inclusion buttressed by the economic development in different countries, except for countries going through political or economic turbulence.
